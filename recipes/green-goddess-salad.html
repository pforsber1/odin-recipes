Green Goddess Salad

Description

The Green Goddess Salad is a splendid mixture of romaine lettuce topped with hearty green such as pistachios, avocados, cucumbers, and green onions. The dish is served with a dressing made with mayonnaise, parsley, spinach, basil, tarragon, and lemon juice. This dressing gives the salad a subtle yet tasteful citric flavor.

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 1/3 cup loosely packed fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped tarragon leaves
  • 1/2 medium shallot, peeled, coarsely chopped
  • 1 samll garlic clove, peeled, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon anchovy paste
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt

Salad

  • 4 cups chopped green cabbage
  • 4 cups chopped hearts of romaine
  • 3 medium Persian cucumbers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 1 medium ripe avocado, peeled, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped roasted, salted pistachios

Steps

Dressing

  1. Combine spinach, parsley, basil, chives, tarragon, shallot, garlic, anchovy paste, and lime juice in a high-powered blender
  2. Blend and slowly add oil through top opening of blender until mixture is smooth, about 1 minute
  3. Add mayonnaise and salt; blend until smooth
  4. Cover and chill until ready to serve

Salad

  1. Combine cabbage, romaine, cucumber, and green onions in a large bowl
  2. Add 2/3 cup of dressing
  3. Top with avocado and pistachios
