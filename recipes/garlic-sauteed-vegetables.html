Garlic Sauteed Vegetables

Description

This rich and savory dish is an assortment of yellow squash, red bell peppers, string green beans, and cauliflower. The vegetables are sauteed until they reach a golden brown and mixed with garlic and broth. This is an easy, hassle-free recipe that is sure to put a smile on everyone at the dinner table!


Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper (1-inch)
  • 1 1/2 cups thickly sliced cauliflower florets
  • 1 medium yellow squash, havled and sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Swanson Chicken Broth

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a 12-inch skillet over high heat.
  2. Add the red pepper, cauliflower, green beans, and squash and cook for 4 minutes or until lightly browned. Stir the dish occasionally to cook evenly.
  3. Stir in the garlic into the skillet and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the Swanson Chicken Broth and heat to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium-high and cook for 3 minutes.
